backend technologies

The backend of a website or application—often referred to as the "server-side"—is the hidden engine that powers everything happening behind the scenes. While the frontend focuses on what the user sees and interacts with, the backend is responsible for storing and organizing data, executing core business logic, and ensuring that everything on the frontend actually works.

databses techstack

At the core of every modern application lies the database, the specialized system responsible for securely storing, organizing, and retrieving vast amounts of digital information. While frontend technologies present the interface and backend logic processes the rules, the database serves as the ultimate source of truth, retaining everything from user profiles and transaction histories to product catalogs and system logs.
